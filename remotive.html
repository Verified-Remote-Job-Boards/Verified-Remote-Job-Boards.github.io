Remotive Review: A Deep Dive into the Premier Remote Tech Job Board

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Verification Score: 92/100

Remotive stands as one of the most established and respected remote job boards, particularly within the technology and startup sectors. Founded in 2014 by Rodolphe Dutel, the platform has cultivated a reputation for curating high-quality, fully remote positions, effectively filtering out the noise and low-quality listings that plague many general job sites. It is a dedicated resource for both job seekers looking for their next remote role and companies aiming to build a global, distributed workforce.

Unlike many competitors that rely on scraping or automated feeds, Remotive focuses on direct employer postings, ensuring a higher degree of job legitimacy and relevance. This commitment to quality over quantity is a core tenet of the platform, making it a valuable resource for experienced professionals in software development, design, marketing, and product management. Our verification process confirms Remotive's legitimacy and its strong standing in the remote work community.

Detailed Features and Job Quality

Remotive's primary strength lies in its meticulous curation process. The platform is heavily skewed towards tech-centric roles, including Software Development, Design, Product, and Marketing. This specialization means that while the total volume of jobs might be lower than a massive aggregator, the signal-to-noise ratio is exceptionally high. Job seekers can trust that the listings are genuinely remote and often come from well-known, remote-first companies like Zapier, Salesforce, and Airtable [1].

Key features that enhance the job search experience include:

  • Advanced Filtering: Users can filter by job category, experience level (entry-level to executive), contract type (full-time, part-time, freelance), and even specific skills (e.g., Python, SQL).
  • Global Reach with North American Focus: While Remotive lists jobs worldwide, approximately 60% of its traffic originates from North America, making it an excellent channel for companies targeting U.S. and Canadian talent [1].
  • Multi-Channel Promotion: Every job posting is actively promoted across multiple channels, including the Remotive newsletter (reaching thousands of subscribers), Twitter, Slack communities, and the Google Jobs Careers Network. This ensures maximum visibility for employers and a constant stream of fresh opportunities for job seekers.
  • Company Profiles: Employers can create detailed company profiles, allowing job seekers to research the company culture, mission, and remote work policies before applying, leading to better-matched candidates.

The quality of jobs is consistently high, with a strong emphasis on roles that offer competitive salaries and benefits. The platform's reputation attracts employers who are serious about remote work, which translates into better opportunities for the job seeker.

Pricing Analysis for Employers

Remotive operates on a paid-posting model, which is a key factor in maintaining the quality of its listings. There is no free job posting option [1]. The pricing structure is straightforward, with a single job posting costing between $299.00 and $448.00, depending on the selected upgrades [1].

Posting Option Price Range Key Features
Standard Post ~$299.00 30-day listing, inclusion in the newsletter, social media promotion.
Upgraded Post Up to $448.00 Includes features like "Featured" status, highlighting, and increased visibility.
Bulk/Non-Profit Custom Pricing Discounts are available for bulk purchases and for non-profit organizations [1].

A significant point for employers to note is the renewal policy: listings must be renewed every 30 days at the full price [1]. While this is a drawback compared to some competitors who offer longer listing periods, it reinforces the platform's focus on keeping the job board fresh and current. The investment is justified by the highly targeted and qualified audience, particularly for hard-to-fill tech roles.

Best For

Remotive is best for established companies and startups that are serious about hiring top-tier, experienced talent in Software Development, Design, and Product Management. It is particularly effective for organizations targeting a North American audience but with a global remote policy. Job seekers who are mid-to-senior level professionals in the tech industry will find Remotive to be one of the most efficient uses of their job search time.

"I was really excited about Remotive based on all the hype, but as a content writer, I was disappointed. The vast majority of the jobs are for software developers, product managers, and designers. I spent a lot of time checking the site only to find maybe one or two relevant jobs a week. It's clearly a tech-first platform, and if you're in a non-technical field like content, HR, or finance, you're much better off using a board that has a broader focus. It's a great site, but it's not for me."

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is Remotive a legitimate job board?

Yes, Remotive is a highly legitimate and well-respected job board founded in 2014. It is known for curating high-quality, fully remote positions, primarily in the tech and startup ecosystem.

2. How much does it cost to post a job on Remotive?

A single job posting on Remotive costs between $299.00 and $448.00, depending on the upgrades selected. There is no free option to post a job [1].

3. Does Remotive focus on specific types of jobs?

Remotive is heavily focused on tech-related roles, including Software Development, Design, Marketing, and Product Management. While other categories exist, the platform's strength is in the tech sector.

4. Is Remotive better for job seekers or employers?

Remotive is an excellent resource for both. Employers benefit from a highly qualified, targeted audience, and job seekers benefit from a curated list of high-quality, legitimate remote roles.

5. How often are new jobs posted on Remotive?

New jobs are posted daily, and the platform actively promotes them through its newsletter and social channels to ensure the listings remain fresh and current.
