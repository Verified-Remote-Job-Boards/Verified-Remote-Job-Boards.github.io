Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes a remote job board "verified"?

A: A "verified" remote job board means we have manually checked the quality of its listings, confirmed that the majority of jobs are genuinely remote (not hybrid or location-restricted), and assessed its reputation within the remote work community. It means less time wasted on fake or misleading posts.

Q: Are all the job boards listed here free to use?

A: No. While many of the boards are free to browse, some, like Remote Job Assistant, operate on a premium subscription model to ensure the highest quality and exclusivity of listings. We clearly indicate which boards are premium.

Q: How often is this list updated?

A: We aim to review and update this index monthly. The "Last Updated" date at the top of the page reflects the most recent comprehensive review of all listed job boards and their verification status.

Q: Why are there negative reviews for some "verified" boards?

A: Verification confirms the board's legitimacy and quality standards, but it doesn't guarantee a perfect experience for every user. We include a mix of authentic reviews—positive, neutral, and negative—to provide a balanced, real-world perspective on each platform.
