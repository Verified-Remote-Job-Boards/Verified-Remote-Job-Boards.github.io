Best Remote Job Boards for Product Managers

Finding a remote Product Manager role can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. While general job boards are flooded with irrelevant listings, the best opportunities are often hidden on specialized platforms. This guide cuts through the noise to present the top remote job boards specifically curated for Product Managers, ensuring you spend less time filtering and more time applying to your dream role.

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Top Remote Job Boards for Product Managers

5. FlexJobs

While the subscription is a barrier, the time saved by avoiding scams and low-quality postings can be worth the cost for serious job seekers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is a Product Manager a good role for remote work?

Yes, Product Management is highly suitable for remote work. The core responsibilities—strategy, communication, documentation, and stakeholder management—are primarily digital and communication-based, making them easily transferable to a remote setting. Many companies, especially in tech, have embraced remote PMs to access a wider talent pool.

Should I use general job boards or specialized remote boards?

It is best to use a combination. Specialized remote boards (like those listed above) offer higher quality, pre-vetted remote roles, reducing the time you spend filtering. General boards (like LinkedIn or Indeed) offer higher volume, but require more effort to filter out irrelevant or hybrid roles. Prioritize the specialized boards first.

What is the average salary for a remote Product Manager?

The salary for a remote Product Manager varies significantly based on experience, location (even for remote roles, companies often adjust pay based on cost of labor in your region), and company size. However, a mid-level remote PM in the US can expect a base salary range of $120,000 to $180,000, with senior and director-level roles exceeding $200,000.
