The Ultimate Guide to the Best Remote Job Boards for Content Writers

Stop sifting through irrelevant listings. We've curated and reviewed the top job boards that specialize in high-quality, remote content and copywriting roles.

Finding Your Next Remote Writing Gig

The demand for skilled remote content writers has never been higher, but so has the noise. General job sites are flooded with low-paying or irrelevant positions. To succeed, you need to focus your search on platforms that understand the content creation industry. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Remote Job Assistant free to use?

Remote Job Assistant offers a free tier for basic job browsing, but its premium features, including AI-powered matching and direct application links, require a paid subscription. Given the quality of the leads, many professional writers find the subscription well worth the investment.

What is the best job board for entry-level content writers?

For entry-level writers, ProBlogger Job Board is often recommended due to its high volume of postings, including many for smaller projects or part-time work that can help build a portfolio. However, be prepared for high competition.

Should I use a general job board like LinkedIn or Indeed?

While general job boards have a massive reach, they require extensive filtering to find quality remote content roles. Specialized boards like Remote Job Assistant and ProBlogger save time and typically feature higher-paying, more relevant positions.
