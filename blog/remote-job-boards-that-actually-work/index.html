Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What makes a remote job board 'actually work'?

A remote job board that 'actually works' is one that curates high-quality, genuinely remote positions, filters out scams and location-specific jobs, and provides a good user experience. The best ones often specialize in remote work, rather than being general job sites with a remote filter.

How often is this list of remote job boards updated?

This guide is meticulously reviewed and updated on a regular basis to ensure all information is current, and new, effective job boards are added. The last update was on January 21, 2026.

Are the job boards listed here free to use for job seekers?

Most of the job boards listed here are completely free for job seekers. Some premium boards may offer optional paid features for enhanced visibility or tools, but the core job search functionality is typically free.

Should I use a general job site (like LinkedIn or Indeed) or a niche remote board?

We recommend starting with niche remote boards or an aggregator like Remote Job Assistant. While general sites have more volume, the signal-to-noise ratio is often poor. Niche boards save you time by pre-filtering for truly remote opportunities.
