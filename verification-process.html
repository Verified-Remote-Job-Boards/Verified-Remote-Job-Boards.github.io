Our 7-Stage Verification Process

Every remote job board we review undergoes our rigorous 7-stage verification process to ensure we're recommending only the most legitimate platforms.

Stage 1: Initial Legitimacy Assessment (3-5 days)

We verify the company's legal registration, leadership team, financial stability, and online presence.

Stage 2: Job Listing Quality Analysis (7-10 days)

Our team manually reviews hundreds of job listings to assess authenticity, currency, and quality.

Stage 3: User Experience Testing (5-7 days)

We evaluate usability, mobile responsiveness, search functionality, and overall user experience.

Stage 4: Company & Employer Verification (10-14 days)

We conduct background checks on 50-100 companies posting on each platform to verify legitimacy.

Stage 5: Pricing and Value Analysis (3-5 days)

We assess cost structure, ROI, transparency, and value for money.

Stage 6: Customer Support Evaluation (5-7 days)

We test response times, quality of support, and availability of resources.

Stage 7: Ongoing Monitoring (Continuous)

We conduct monthly checks, gather user feedback, and perform annual re-verification.

Our Commitment: We maintain editorial independence and are not paid by job boards for reviews. Our scores are based solely on our comprehensive evaluation criteria.
