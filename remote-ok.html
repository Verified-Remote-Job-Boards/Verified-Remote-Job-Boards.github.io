Our Verdict on Remote OK

Remote OK, founded by Pieter Levels, is one of the original and most respected remote job boards in the industry. It has built a reputation for high-quality, genuinely remote positions, primarily in the tech sector. Unlike some general job sites, Remote OK is laser-focused on connecting skilled professionals with companies that embrace a remote-first culture.

Our comprehensive review for 2026 finds that while the volume of listings is lower than massive aggregators, the signal-to-noise ratio is excellent. It remains a top-tier resource, especially for developers, designers, and marketers looking for global opportunities. We rate it 4.2 out of 5.0, a strong recommendation for serious remote job seekers.

Pros and Cons of Remote OK

What We Love (Pros)

  • High-quality, genuinely remote job listings.
  • Strong focus on tech roles (Software, Design, Marketing).
  • Clean, minimalist interface that is easy to navigate.
  • Excellent filtering options, including salary and time zone.
  • Global job coverage, not limited to a single country.

Areas for Improvement (Cons)

  • Lower volume of listings compared to general job aggregators.
  • Less variety in non-tech roles (e.g., HR, Finance).
  • Jobs can be highly competitive due to the site's popularity.
  • Some listings are for contract work, not full-time employment.
  • No built-in application tracking system.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Remote OK free for job seekers?

Yes, Remote OK is completely free for job seekers. You can browse all listings, set up email alerts, and apply to jobs without any cost. The platform generates revenue by charging companies to post job listings.

What types of remote jobs are most common on Remote OK?

Remote OK is heavily focused on technical roles, with a strong emphasis on Software Development, Design, and Marketing. You will also find positions in Customer Support, Sales, and non-tech categories, but the majority of listings cater to the tech industry.

How often are new jobs posted on Remote OK?

New jobs are posted daily, often multiple times a day. The platform is highly active, which is one of its main strengths. However, due to its popularity, jobs can fill up quickly, so checking frequently is recommended.
