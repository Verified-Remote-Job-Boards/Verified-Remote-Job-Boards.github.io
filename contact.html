Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How is Remote Job Assistant different from other job boards?

We focus exclusively on verified, high-quality remote job listings. Our platform is curated to filter out scams and low-effort postings, saving you time and ensuring you only see legitimate opportunities from reputable companies.

Is there a cost to use Remote Job Assistant?

No, our core job search platform is completely free for job seekers. We may offer premium features or resources in the future, but the main job board will always be accessible at no charge.

How often are the job listings updated?

Our listings are updated multiple times throughout the day, seven days a week. We use a combination of automated systems and manual verification to ensure the freshest and most accurate data possible.

Can I post a job opening on Remote Job Assistant?

Yes, we welcome employers looking to hire remote talent. Please use the contact form above to inquire about our job posting options and rates. We'll get back to you with the necessary information.
