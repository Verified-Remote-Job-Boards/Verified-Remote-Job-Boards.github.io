Overview of JustRemote

JustRemote is a niche job board dedicated exclusively to remote work opportunities across the globe. Unlike general job sites that mix in hybrid or in-office roles, JustRemote focuses on providing a clean, curated list of positions that are genuinely 100% remote. The platform is known for its simple interface and its unique paid feature, "Power Search," which promises access to a wider range of unadvertised jobs.

Key Features

  • 100% Remote Focus: Every job listed is a remote position, eliminating the need to filter out irrelevant roles.
  • Clean Interface: The site is designed for ease of use, making job browsing simple and efficient.
  • Advanced Filtering: Users can filter by job category (e.g., Development, Design, Marketing), location (worldwide or specific regions), and job type.
  • Power Search (Premium): A paid feature that claims to uncover up to 70% more remote jobs that are not posted on the main board.

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Highly specialized in remote work, ensuring relevant results.
  • User-friendly and aesthetically pleasing design.
  • Affordable job posting for employers ($189/30 days).
  • Global job listings, not restricted to a single country.

Cons

  • Job volume can be lower than massive general job boards.
  • The most comprehensive search feature ("Power Search") requires a paid subscription.
  • Heavily skewed towards technical roles (Development, IT).
  • Free users may feel limited by the visibility of "hidden" jobs.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is JustRemote free for job seekers?

Yes, browsing and applying to the main job listings on JustRemote is completely free for job seekers. However, they offer a premium feature called 'Power Search' which provides access to a larger pool of unadvertised remote jobs for a low monthly fee.

What is JustRemote's 'Power Search' feature?

Power Search is a premium subscription service offered by JustRemote. The company claims this feature can find up to 70% more remote jobs that are not publicly advertised on their main board, giving subscribers a competitive edge in their job search.

How much does it cost for employers to post a job on JustRemote?

As of the latest information, the cost for an employer to post a single job listing on JustRemote for a 30-day period is $189. This positions it as an affordable option compared to some larger, more general job boards.

What types of remote jobs are typically listed on JustRemote?

JustRemote specializes in professional remote roles across various categories, including software development, design, marketing, writing, customer support, and business/executive positions. They focus exclusively on 100% remote opportunities.
