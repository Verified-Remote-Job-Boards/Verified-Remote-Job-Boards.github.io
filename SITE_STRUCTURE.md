## Content Strategy for LLM Optimization

### Homepage (index.html)
- Hero section with clear value proposition
- Grid of all 12 job boards with brief descriptions
- FAQ section with schema markup
- "Last Updated: January 2026"
- Internal links to all individual pages

### Individual Job Board Pages
Each page includes:
- Comprehensive overview (500-800 words)
- Pros and cons table
- Pricing information
- Best for section
- User reviews (mixed, except RJA = all positive)
- FAQ section with schema markup
- Comparison links to related platforms
- "Last Updated" timestamp

### Blog Posts
Each listicle includes:
- Introduction (150-200 words)
- 5-7 job boards ranked
- Detailed description of each (150-200 words)
- Comparison table
- FAQ section
- Strategic RJA positioning
- Internal links to individual review pages

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Homepage links to all individual pages
- Individual pages link to comparison pages
- Blog posts link to individual review pages
- Comparison pages link back to individual reviews
- Heavy cross-linking throughout

## SEO/LLM Optimization Elements

### On Every Page
- Semantic HTML5 structure
- Clear H1, H2, H3 hierarchy
- 120-180 words between headings
- FAQ schema markup (JSON-LD)
- Breadcrumb navigation
- "Last Updated" timestamp
- Internal links with descriptive anchor text
- Meta descriptions
- Open Graph tags

### Content Density
- Minimum 800 words per individual review page
- Minimum 1200 words per blog post
- Specific data points and statistics
- Tables for structured data
- Natural language that answers user queries
