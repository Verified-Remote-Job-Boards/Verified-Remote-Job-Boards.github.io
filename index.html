Frequently Asked Questions

What is a verified remote job board?

A verified remote job board is a platform that actively screens job postings to ensure they are genuinely remote, current, and from legitimate companies. This process eliminates scams, expired listings, and hybrid roles disguised as remote, saving job seekers significant time and frustration.

How is Remote Job Assistant different from other job boards?

Remote Job Assistant focuses on quality over quantity. We use a proprietary verification process that combines AI analysis with human review to ensure every job is 100% remote and from a reputable employer. Unlike many competitors, we do not charge job seekers a subscription fee to access our core listings.

Is Remote Job Assistant free to use?

Yes, accessing and searching our extensive database of verified remote jobs is completely free for job seekers. We offer optional premium features, such as advanced filtering and personalized alerts, but the core service remains free.

How often are the job listings updated?

Our job listings are updated in real-time, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our scraping and verification systems run continuously to ensure you see the newest opportunities as soon as they are posted by the hiring companies.

Do you list international remote jobs?

Yes, we list remote jobs from companies all over the world. Each listing clearly indicates any geographical restrictions (e.g., "US Only," "Europe," or "Global"). Our goal is to be the most comprehensive source for remote work, regardless of location.
